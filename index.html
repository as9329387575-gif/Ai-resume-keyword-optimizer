What are resume keywords and why ATS matters

Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS) scan resumes for specific keywords related to skills, tools, certifications, and soft skills. If your resume lacks these, it may be filtered out before a recruiter sees it. Our AI Resume Keyword Optimizer helps you identify missing terms and improve your match rate.

How ATS scans resumes

ATS software parses your resume and extracts nouns, skill phrases, and industry buzzwords. It compares them against the job description. Using a resume keyword optimizer ensures you speak the same language as the employer.

Common resume keyword mistakes

Over-stuffing keywords, missing important variations, or not including both hard and soft skills. Our tool highlights overused words and recommends high-impact keywords.
